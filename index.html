ABOUT US

Sreedhareeyam Convention Centre

Welcome to Sreedhareeyam Convention Centre, one of the leading venues in Karunagappally, Kollam, designed to host your most memorable events. Whether it's a grand wedding, a professional conference, or a cultural celebration, we provide elegant, spacious, and well-equipped halls tailored to your needs. With a commitment to quality, affordability, and service excellence, we offer a wide range of facilities including AC halls, banquet spaces, auditoriums, and Kalyana Mandapams.
