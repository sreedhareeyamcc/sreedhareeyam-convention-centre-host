Our Facilities

At Sreedhareeyam Convention Centre, we ensure every event is supported by world-class amenities and thoughtful infrastructure to provide a truly exceptional experience.

Centralized Air Conditioning

All our event spaces, including auditoriums and banquet halls, are fully air-conditioned. We maintain optimal climate control for comfort during all seasons. Advanced ventilation systems ensure fresh air circulation at all times. Temperature settings can be adjusted based on event requirements.

Comfortable Seating

We provide modern and ergonomic seating arrangements tailored to the type of event you're hosting. From formal business setups to lavish wedding receptions, our seating options ensure both style and comfort for all attendees, ensuring a great experience even during long events.

Ample Parking Area

We offer a large parking space that can accommodate over 200 vehicles, ensuring hassle-free parking for all your guests. Separate entry and exit points are designed for smooth traffic flow. Dedicated staff is available to assist with parking during peak events. Valet services can be arranged on request.

Well-Maintained Restrooms

Our clean and hygienic restrooms are regularly maintained to meet high sanitation standards. Separate facilities for men and women are available on every floor, along with wheelchair-accessible restrooms to provide comfort and convenience for all guests.

Lift/Elevator Access

For easy and convenient access across all floors, our facility is equipped with modern elevators. These lifts are maintained regularly and are designed to accommodate guests of all ages, making movement seamless for everyone, including elderly or differently-abled visitors.
