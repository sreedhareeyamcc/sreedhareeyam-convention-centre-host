Welcome to Sreedhareeyam Convention Centre, one of the leading venues in Karunagappally, Kollam, designed to host your most memorable events. Whether it's a grand wedding, a professional conference, or a cultural celebration, we provide elegant, spacious, and well-equipped halls tailored to your needs. With a commitment to quality, affordability, and service excellence, we offer a wide range of facilities including AC halls, banquet spaces, auditoriums, and Kalyana Mandapams. Backed by a team of experienced professionals, we ensure every event is smooth, successful, and stress-free.

Our centre is fully equipped with modern amenities, including 2 lifts, 2 escalators, centralized air conditioning, ample parking, and high-end AV systems, making it a preferred choice for both personal and corporate gatherings.
